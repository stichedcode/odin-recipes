Simple Sugar Cookies

Sugar Cookie Recipe Overview

Sometimes when you're craving a sugary treat there's nothing better than a simple sugar cookie!

This cookie classic is simple to make and even simpler to enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Baking Spray
  • 2 3/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1 cup of softened butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all the needed ingredients and preheat the oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Stir the flour, baking soda, and baking powder in a small bowl.
  3. Beat the sugar and butter together in a large bowl using an electric mixer until smooth.
  4. Beat the egg and vanilla into this mixture.
  5. Gradually add in the flour mixture from step 2.
  6. Roll the dough into walnut-sized balls and place them onto ungreased baking sheets about 2 inches apart.
  7. Bake in the oven for about 8 to 10 minutes, until the edges are golden brown. Cool them on baking sheets before moving them to a wire rack to cool completely.
