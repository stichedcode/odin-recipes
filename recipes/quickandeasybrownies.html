Quick and Easy Brownie Recipe

Brownie Recipe Overview

This brownie recipe creates chocolatey delicious batches of square heaven!

If you need a treat to bring to an upcoming holiday party, this quick and easy recipe will knock the socks off you and the other party guests!

Ingredients

  • Baking Spray
  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
  • 1 cup of melted unsalted butter
  • 4 large room temperature eggs
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of walnut halves

Steps

  1. Gather all the needed ingredients.
  2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Grease a 9 by 13 inch pan with the baking spray.
  3. Whisk the sugar, flour, melted butter, eggs, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until everything is combined.
  4. Spread the batter into the greased pan.
  5. Sprinkle the top with the walnut halves.
  6. Bake the brownies for 20 to 30 minutes, until the top looks crinkled and a toothpick comes out with a few moist crumbs.
  7. Let the brownies cool in the pan on a wire rack before slicing into squares and serving.
